Title: Idea 2 Product Discussion


  • As discussed with product team, InfoSec will be involved in i2p process for any critical features where Infosec opinion is required
  • Participants of i2p meeting will be: Technical Architect + Product Manager + Designer + Security Team (Optional , Will be included for critical features)
  • Infosec team will deliver security checklist against the feature solution discussed in i2p meeting
  • Feature validation against high level security check-list should be done in PM review and break-down the same as Test cases for QA/Security test in staging environment
  • As we progress, Freshsales team will include 'Security' user story or Sub-Task in their workflow
