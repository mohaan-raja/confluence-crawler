Program Summary

CPQ (Configure, Price, Quote) will be an add on product in Freshsales to enable Freshsales customers to create products and quotations. Entire development program has been divided into two parts. On a high level those are given below.

  1. CPQ V1 which includes the ability to  create products in Freshsales . Epic link - https://freshworks.freshrelease.com/FSALES/issues/FSALES-14008
  2. CPQ V2 which includes the ability to create quotations and offer it to customers. Epic link - https://freshworks.freshrelease.com/FSALES/issues/FSALES-15661
