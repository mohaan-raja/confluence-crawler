1. Coverity (SAST) and AdvisoryBot (SCA) capability are integrated into the product's CI to identify and track security bugs to closure in Freshrelease


2. Authzchecker (DAST) is integrated into the product's CD pipeline to identify and track security bugs to closure in Freshrelease

5. Threat modeling/security design review is conducted by the respective product teams for features or functionalities that require security considerations
