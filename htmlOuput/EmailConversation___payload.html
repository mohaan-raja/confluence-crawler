Title: EmailConversation - payload

Since the frequency of these events are very rare, Initially, we onboard the events without truncating the payload. However, we will introduce new alerts to monitor the frequency of payloads that exceed the limit.
In scenarios where the customer expects the event or is based on specific use cases, we can introduce truncation on the payload of these events.

It was agreed upon that if the payload size exceeds 255KB, it should be dropped.
