Title: AU Pod Testing

Testing Aspects

  • Run a full automation suite on the new pod and check for the failures
  • Create a new account and do a sanity check.
  • Migrate an existing Account (US, Canary, EU) pod account to the new AU pod and do a sanity check on migrated account
  • Check for below mentioned  services, integration and marketplace application
