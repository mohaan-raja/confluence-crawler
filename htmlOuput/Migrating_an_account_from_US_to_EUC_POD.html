Title: Migrating an account from US to EUC POD

New Detailed Steps POD Migration ( L5 tickets ) - last Updated on 16 Dec 2023 

OLD SCRIPT & STEPS

Make sure to inform the customer that there will be downtime during migration.

  1. In US POD
    1. SSH into any one of the background or data-migration instance
    2. Traverse to Rails.root
    3. Run: RAILS_ENV=production bundle exec rake migrate_account:dump[<account_id>]
    4. Wait for the dump to S3 to complete.
  2. In EUC POD
    1. SSH into bastion
    2. AWS_ACCESS_KEY_ID=<access_key_id from s3.yml> AWS_SECRET_ACCESS_KEY=<secret_access_key from s3.yml> aws s3 cp s3://cdn.freshsales.io/<reverse-of-account-id>/ s3://cdn-fsales-euc.freshsales.io/<reverse-of-account-id>/ --recursive --sse AES256 --acl private
    3. AWS_ACCESS_KEY_ID=<access_key_id from s3.yml> AWS_SECRET_ACCESS_KEY=<secret_access_key from s3.yml> aws s3 cp s3://email-content-us-2020/<account_id>/ s3://email-content-euc-2020/<account_id>/ --recursive --sse AES256 --acl private
      and repeat for all other years

    4. SSH into background instance

    5. Run: RAILS_ENV=production bundle exec rake migrate_account:load[<account id>,<requester email>]
