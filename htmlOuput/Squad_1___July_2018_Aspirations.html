Task Performance changes

For the past few months, We have had complaints from customers and internal team, that My tasks and appointments page is either too slow or not loading at all. This was because the way it was designed back in 2016.

When you land in My tasks and appointments page, we load tasks that you are owner or that you are collaborator. But this query was doing a left join on tasks and task users with OR condition in where. Also it was not picking the indexes as well. This was a bad approach.

So we started questioning the approach and completely rethought it. We decided that owner of a task is collaborator by default. This simplified things wherein we can do away from OR condition. We shipped this today morning around 6.30AM and was happy to see the improvement in newrelic.



At one point, we have had 280ms for task select query. Now it is executing around 0.6ms.

Before 6.30AM, average response time was 776ms. Now it's at 92ms.

Good work Buden, Irfan and Praveen!
