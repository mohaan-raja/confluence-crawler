Title: Rspec coverage initiative

Planned completion date to reach 100 % coverage is by end of December 2019. Plan suggested based on the estimate provided by all squads is to have one dedicated resource working on Rspec all time till Dec end. However squad is free to determine the course of action such as having couple of resource towards end of sprint, having one resource on a rotational basis etc.
