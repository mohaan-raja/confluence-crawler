Title: Labels for other phone numbers


We had discussed the following:
1. Understand Freshsales's implementation of other phone numbers. 
2. Identify any gaps in the implementation of other phone numbers in Freshcaller and MCR. 

The following decisions are taken:

  1. Mobile and Work need to be kept as separate fields and other phone number field is used to capture other phone numbers with labels. These need to remain as 3 separate fields as there are workflows configured based on Work and Mobile. 
  2. Freshcaller to sync these other phone numbers with their respective label IDs
